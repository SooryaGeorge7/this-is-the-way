How to Play

Start the game by pressing the Play game button on the start screen.

Use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move Grogu left and right.

Use the up arrow key to make Mario jump. You can jump on top of enemies/Jawa to defeat them or jump over obstacles.

Collect frogs by eating them. Each frog gives you 1 life.

Grogu must kill the Jawas to gain points.

If grogu's health declines to 0%, then grogu dies.

Get through all the levels so that grogu can get to Mandolorian.

You win if you get to Mandolorian without being killed!
